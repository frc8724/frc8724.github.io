Our FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team, Mayhem, welcomes students ages 14-18.

Program Features: Student-led training with adult mentor oversight in mechanical engineering, software engineering, power tools, collaboration, strategy, teamwork and business management.

Our year-round program includes:
January-April: FRC build season and competitions
May-August: Post season retrospective, recruiting, skill enhancement, outreach, and fundraising
September-November: Offseason competitions, continued outreach and fundraising.

Please email us if you have any questions or are interested in participating!
